About PetHub

PetHub is a practical pet care system built around real features, not empty promises.

A real platform for pet care management

PetHub was developed to help pet owners and administrators manage animals, appointments, and health records in one place.

The system uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, and secure record retrieval, while Node and Express power the API behind the dashboard.

Users can register, verify their email, manage pets, book appointments, and review care records without leaving the platform.

What PetHub includes

Pet records

Complete pet profiles with medical notes, weight, vaccine details, and care instructions.

Appointments

Book and review appointments, then update status from the admin dashboard.

Admin management

Admins can access user lists, pet lists, appointment overviews, and analytics summary endpoints.

How the system works

PetHub is designed around the actual workflows needed for pet care: account creation, verification, secure records, and meaningful admin oversight.

Register

Create your account and verify your email to start using the app.

Manage pets

Add pets, update care records, and keep their health history organized.

Book appointments

Schedule visits and review appointment statuses directly in the dashboard.
